How to Install

  • Use any one of the links from above to download the profile to your phone. (Tip: Use your phones Safari browser for the task. If you are using any other browser, switch to the Safari and open the URL ‘https://topstore.download/download‘ and repeat from step 2)
  • In the installation pop-up window tap ‘Install’ to begin.
  • Give it a few seconds to finish the installation. During the installation, you can see the app icon is less bright than the other.
  • Don’t open the app yet.
  • Navigate to the following path in your iPhone. ‘Settings > General > Profile & Device Management’
  • Click ‘Trust’ on the TopStore app profile. This can solve the ‘Untrusted Developer Error’.
  • Good job! Now you have TopStore on your iPhone.

If you are still struggling to install, you can find help in the troubleshoot section.
